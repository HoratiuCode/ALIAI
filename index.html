Welcome to ALIAI

Clean old files fast.

ALIAI scans your Documents, Pictures, Downloads, Desktop, and app folders to find files and software you have not used in a while, then lets you move selected items to Trash.

python3 aliai.py --large-scan --time-preset very-long --limit 50

Defaults

  • Scans: ~/Documents, ~/Pictures, ~/Downloads, ~/Desktop
  • Large scan adds: ~/Movies, ~/Music, ~/Public
  • Apps: ~/Applications and /Applications
  • Age threshold: 180 days

Useful Commands

  • python3 aliai.py for a standard scan
  • python3 aliai.py --age 1y for older files
  • python3 aliai.py --large-scan for a broader scan
  • python3 aliai.py --time-preset very-long for a 2-year scan
  • python3 aliai.py --software-only --age 1y to find old software only
  • python3 aliai.py --delete to jump into deletion mode
  • python3 aliai.py --no-apps to skip app bundle scanning
Minimal by design. Built for quick cleanup.
